Hi! I’m Alyssa, a journalist and podcaster with more than 10 years of experience. I write about media, celebrity, technology, and how the internet is changing our lives. My reporting has taken me everywhere from Apple product launches to the 2016 primaries to Southern California succulent farms. I’m based in Los Angeles as a staffer at The Ringer, where I reported, wrote, and hosted Boom/Bust: HQ Trivia, a 2020 audio documentary about the rise and fall of a viral trivia app. At the moment I’m working on a new show, slated to come out this year.
